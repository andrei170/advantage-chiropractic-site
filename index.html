Our Story

A Green Bay chiropractic clinic, on the same street, for 33 years.

Advantage Chiropractic Clinic of Green Bay opened in 1993 at 1804 South Ashland Avenue, and the clinic has cared for patients from that same address ever since. Thirty-three years of independent practice in a city that has watched chiropractic chain franchises arrive, expand, and standardize the patient experience.

The continuity is part of the value. A patient who came in for a low-back consultation in 2003 can return in 2026 and find the same independent clinic, the same commitment to matching technique to the individual case, and a team that has worked together long enough to share notes across the case roster.

"Three doctors. A DABCI internist. A Webster-certified prenatal specialist. A four-technique chiropractic stack. All on South Ashland Avenue, independently owned since 1993."
  • Independent ownership. Locally owned and operated, not a franchise location.
  • Same address since 1993. Thirty-three years on South Ashland Avenue.
  • 10 Best of The Bay wins. Community-voted, Press-Gazette readership, 2016 to 2025.
  • Three doctors, one clinic. Dr. Metzler, Dr. Swanson, and Dr. Kirchner share cases.
  • Decisions made here. Care plans are set inside the clinic, not by a franchise office.

A decade of recognition

Ten consecutive Best of The Bay wins. Voted by Green Bay.

Best of The Bay is the Green Bay Press-Gazette's community-voted recognition program, decided by readership ballot each year. Ten years in a row is exceptional in any category, and in chiropractic it is an unbroken streak that spans a generation of practitioners. The clinic won it the first year. Then again. Then again, every year from 2016 through 2025.

"The chiropractic clinic the Press-Gazette readership has picked Best of The Bay every year for a decade. A community vote, not a sponsored list."

For a patient choosing between a locally owned independent clinic and a national franchise, the Best of The Bay streak is the kind of track record that speaks directly. It is not a directory ranking or an algorithm. It is your neighbors voting, year after year, for the same clinic.

Prenatal and Pediatric

Webster Technique Prenatal Chiropractic

Dr. Jon Swanson is certified in the Webster Technique, a recognized prenatal chiropractic certification recognized by the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association. A small number of Webster-certified chiropractors practice in the Green Bay metro.

  • Webster Technique for prenatal patients

  • FMT and Rocktape credentials
  • Family chiropractic care
Chiropractic Internal Medicine

DABCI Internal Medicine Consultation

Dr. Cheryl Metzler holds the DABCI diplomate, a post-graduate credential from the American Board of Chiropractic Internists that qualifies her to consult on internal-disorder presentations alongside spinal care. Held by a short list of chiropractors in Wisconsin, statewide.

  • Internal-disorder consultation alongside spinal care
  • DABCI diplomate, American Board of Chiropractic Internists
  • Dr. Metzler trained at Northwestern College of Chiropractic (1988)
  • Scope reviewed against ABCI certification guidelines
Four-Technique Stack

Gonstead, Diversified, Activator, and Cox Flexion Distraction

Dr. Alexander Kirchner works across four distinct chiropractic techniques. That range means the technique can be matched to what your case actually requires, rather than every patient receiving the same standard adjustment.

Prenatal and Pediatric Chiropractic Specialist

The Webster Technique specialist for Green Bay families.

Dr. Swanson graduated from Northwestern Health Sciences University in 2015 and is certified in the Webster Technique, recognized by the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association for prenatal chiropractic care. He also holds FMT and Rocktape credentials. A small set of Webster-certified chiropractors practice in the Green Bay metro.

  • Certification: Webster Technique (ICPA-recognized)
  • Additional: FMT (Functional Movement Techniques), Rocktape
  • Training: Northwestern Health Sciences University, 2015
  • Focus: Prenatal patients, families, pediatric chiropractic
